About Us

We are a team of high school students passionate about making mental health support more accessible to everyone. Project Sigmund is our attempt to use AI to help people talk about their problems in a safe, affordable way.

  • Our Mission: Make therapy accessible for all, regardless of income.
  • Our Team: Driven, creative, and committed to positive change.
  • Our Tech: Combining AI, speech recognition, and a friendly interface.

We value your privacy just as we value our transparency so check out our GitHub repository for yourselves!

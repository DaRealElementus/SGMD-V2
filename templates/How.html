How Does Sigmund Work?

Sigmund is an AI-powered chat application designed to provide accessible mental health support. Here’s a simple breakdown of how it works:

  • 1. Secure Login & Registration:
    Users create an account or log in. Passwords and usernames are hashed for security before being sent to the server.
  • 2. Encrypted Conversations:
    All chat history is encrypted using AES encryption, with keys derived from your credentials. Only you (with your password) can decrypt your chat history.
  • 3. AI Chatbot:
    When you send a message, Sigmund’s backend uses a local AI model to generate a helpful, supportive response. The AI considers your previous conversation (history) to provide context-aware replies.
  • 4. Privacy First:
    Your chat data is never shared or used for advertising. Everything is stored securely on the server, and only you can access your conversation history.
  • 5. Open Source:
    The entire project is open source. You can view the code, contribute, or run your own instance. See our GitHub repository.

Technical Highlights:

  • Built with Python, Flask, and Dear PyGui for the interface.
  • Uses SQLite for storing user accounts and encrypted chat history.
  • AI responses are generated by a local language model (not cloud-based).
  • All sensitive data is hashed and encrypted before storage or transmission.

We value your privacy just as we value our transparency so check out our GitHub repository for yourselves!

Welcome to Sigmund!

Sigmund is a conversational AI designed to help you talk through your thoughts and feelings in a supportive environment.

Our mission is to make mental health support more accessible, especially for those who may not have access to traditional therapy due to cost or other barriers.

Sigmund is developed by a dedicated team of high school students passionate about technology and mental health.

Feel free to explore the site to learn more about Sigmund, how it works, and how you can benefit from it.
